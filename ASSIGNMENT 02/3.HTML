Our Target & Mission

At KARMA, we are committed to empowering local communities to take an active role in tree planting and forest cleaning. Our mission is to foster environmental stewardship by engaging individuals, schools, and local groups in hands-on conservation efforts.

🌱 Our Target:

  • Restore degraded landscapes by planting thousands of trees annually.
  • Mobilize volunteers to remove waste and invasive species from forests.
  • Educate communities on the importance of sustainable forestry practices.

🌎 Our Mission:

We strive to create a greener, healthier planet by equipping communities with the knowledge, resources, and opportunities to make a lasting environmental impact. Through collaborative action, we aim to restore natural habitats, combat climate change, and promote biodiversity for future generations.

Join us in making a difference—one tree, one cleanup, one community at a time! 🌳🤝
