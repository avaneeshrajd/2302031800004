SERVICES

At KARMA, we offer hands-on environmental initiatives that bring people together to restore and protect nature.

Our services are designed to create lasting ecological and social impact through tree planting and forest cleaning efforts.

🌱 Tree Planting Programs

Organizing community-driven tree planting events

Providing native saplings and sustainable planting techniques

Partnering with schools, businesses, and local groups to increase green cover

🌍 Forest Cleaning Campaigns

Conducting cleanup drives to remove waste and pollutants from forests

Promoting responsible waste disposal and recycling

Educating communities on the importance of preserving natural habitats

🤝 Community Engagement & Education

Workshops on sustainable living and environmental conservation

Training programs for volunteers and local leaders

Educational resources for schools and organizations

💚 Corporate & NGO Partnerships

Collaborative environmental projects for businesses and nonprofits

CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) programs focused on reforestation and conservation

Sponsorship and donation opportunities to support large-scale initiatives
